అః

About అః (Visarga)

అః represents a **breathy “h” sound** at the end of a syllable. It appears mostly in Sanskrit-origin words used in Telugu.

Examples

  • దుఃఖం (Sorrow)
  • శిః (Head — rare)
  • విః (Special prefix — rare)
  • ప్రతిః (Response — rare)
