ళ

About ళ

ళ is a **retroflex ‘la’ sound**. It appears in some Telugu and Sanskrit-origin words, but rarely at the beginning.

Examples (within words)

  • పళని (Fruit pulp)
  • బిళ్ళ (Small plate / token)
  • గుళ్ళు (Bubbles)
  • తాళం (Lock)
