ఱ

About ఱ

ఱ is a **retroflex ‘ra’ sound**. It is used in classical Telugu and some dialects, but rarely starts modern words.

Examples (within words)

  • పఱుగు (Run — dialectal)
  • కొఱకు (For the sake of)
  • తఱచూ (Often)
  • పఱమ (Ultimate — poetic)
